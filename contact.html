Frequently Asked

How long does shipping take?

Standard delivery across India takes 4–7 business days. Express options available at checkout.

What is your return policy?

We offer 7-day hassle-free returns on apparel. Equipment can be returned if unused and in original packaging.

Do you offer size exchanges?

Yes! If the size isn't right, contact us and we'll exchange it for you within 7 days of delivery.

Is there a minimum order for free shipping?

Free shipping on all orders above ₹999. Below that, a flat ₹99 shipping fee applies.
